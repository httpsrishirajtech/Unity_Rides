The Problem

Urban India is broken.
We're fixing it.

Indian cities face a perfect storm of congestion, pollution, and expensive last-mile gaps. Unity Rides tackles all four at once.

🚗
Traffic Gridlock
Millions of hours lost daily to congestion in Indian metros, crippling productivity and quality of life.
✓ E-bikes zip through traffic in dedicated lanes, cutting commute times by up to 60%.
💸
High Transport Costs
Fuel, autos, and cabs drain wallets — especially for students and daily wage workers.
✓ Pay-per-ride from ₹5/km or monthly plans as low as ₹299.
😤
Last-Mile Disconnect
Short 1–5 km trips are awkward — too close for cabs, too far to walk.
✓ Docking stations at Metro exits, colleges & offices for seamless connectivity.
🌫️
Air Pollution Crisis
India holds 9 of the world's 10 most polluted cities. Every petrol vehicle adds to the crisis.
✓ 100% electric fleet. Zero tailpipe emissions. Pure, clean urban air.
